Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

Video2PDF ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Video2PDF collects minimal data to provide our service:

  • Device Information: We collect a random device identifier to manage your subscription and enforce rate limits. This identifier is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Video & Image Data: When you record a video, all frame extraction and image processing happens on your device. Videos and images are never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly use the cloud enhancement feature (free tier).
  • Cloud Enhancement: If you use server-side image enhancement, your images are temporarily uploaded, processed, and the originals are deleted immediately after processing. Enhanced images are stored temporarily and removed within 24 hours.
  • Subscription Data: We process subscription transactions through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment details.
  • Crash & Analytics Data: We collect anonymous crash reports and usage analytics to improve the app. No personal information is included.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the Video2PDF service
  • Process and enhance your book page images
  • Manage your subscription status
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Enforce usage limits for free-tier users
  • Respond to support requests

3. Data Storage & Security

  • On-Device Processing: The majority of image processing happens locally on your device. Your videos and generated PDFs are stored only on your device.
  • Cloud Processing: Temporarily uploaded images are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and processed in memory. Source images are deleted immediately after enhancement. Output images are deleted within 24 hours.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Video2PDF integrates with:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — subscription management
  • Sentry — anonymous crash reporting
  • Cloud infrastructure providers — image processing (encrypted, temporary)

5. Children's Privacy

Video2PDF is designed for students and educators. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app can be used by students of all ages as we collect minimal data that is not personally identifiable.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data by contacting support
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics in your device settings
  • Portability: Export your PDFs at any time from the app

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@video2pdf.ai.

7. Data Retention

  • On-device data: Stored until you delete the app or clear cache
  • Cloud-processed images: Deleted within 24 hours
  • Subscription records: Retained as required by Apple/Google
  • Crash reports: Retained for 90 days

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app. Continued use of Video2PDF after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us
